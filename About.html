About Me

My name is Ray Decker Jr., also known by people on the internet as RVY. I am a self-taught programmer as well as a self-proclaimed "coding wizard." I began teaching myself programming when I was fourteen years old. I read a book on game development and game engine development from cover to cover. I used my love for gaming to fuel my passion for programming and computer languages in a lifelong journey to expand my skills.

My family has been restricted to DSL internet speed, and the internet was nothing like how it is today. Back then, Youtube wasn't loaded with millions of videos on how to write "Hello World" in C++, Udemy hadn't been invented, and the place to learn to program, that is, if you weren't in college, was either by self-discipline, failure, and practice, or hours of reading, researching, failure, and practice.

I didn't start to take my abilities seriously until I discovered the Unity Game Engine. With the help of Udemy and Youtube, I developed my skills in Javascript (when it was supported) and C#. I learned C++ on and off with Visual Basic, developing software for "independent persons" as well as practicing with the Unreal Engine. Java came easier to me than C++, but all of my programming skills came with hard work and years of boot camp-style education.

I am currently working on several individual projects in various programming languages. Please check out my GitHub for some of my more basic programs.
